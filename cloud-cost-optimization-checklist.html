Cloud Cost Optimization Checklist (Mid-Market 2026)

Rightsizing

  • Identify over-provisioned instances (low CPU/memory utilization over 14+ days).
  • Downsize or switch to burstable/spot where workload allows.
  • Review RDS/database instance and storage sizing; check for unused replicas.

Storage

  • Move cold data to cheaper tiers (S3 IA/Glacier, GCS Nearline/Coldline, Azure Cool/Archive).
  • Delete unattached volumes, old snapshots, and abandoned buckets.
  • Turn on lifecycle policies for logs and temp data.

Egress & data transfer

  • Review egress by region and service; see how to reduce egress costs (CDN/WAF).
  • Use CDN for static assets; consider WAF/edge where it cuts origin load.

Reservations & commitments

  • Check Reserved Instance / Savings Plans / committed use coverage vs. baseline usage.
  • Align commitment term with actual runway (1-year vs 3-year).

Quick wins

  • Turn off dev/test outside business hours (scheduler or auto-shutdown).
  • Tag resources for cost allocation and showback.
  • Set billing alerts and anomaly detection.
