Best Managed Hosting for Agencies (2026)

Cloudways vs Kinsta for multiple client sites, WordPress, and high-traffic workloads.

Agencies need managed hosting that scales across many client sites, keeps performance high, and doesn’t require a full-time ops team. In 2026 the two names we recommend most are Cloudways and Kinsta. Which is best depends on whether you’re mostly WordPress or a mix of custom apps. See our Cloudways vs Kinsta: DevOps vs WordPress and the full comparison table.

What agencies need

  • Multiple sites/apps without managing bare metal
  • Staging, one-click or simple deploy, and backups
  • Support that understands WordPress or full-stack
  • Predictable cost and room to grow

Cloudways for agencies

Cloudways gives you managed VPS (DigitalOcean, AWS, GCP) with multiple applications per server, SSH access, and flexible scaling. Good for agencies that run a mix of WordPress and custom stacks (e.g. Laravel, Node). You get dedicated resources, advanced caching, and optional Cloudflare. Strong fit if you need full-stack control and multi-cloud options. Cloudways Blueprint.

Kinsta for agencies

Kinsta is WordPress-first: optimized stack, staging, and WP-savvy support. Ideal for agencies that primarily host client WordPress sites (content, WooCommerce, membership). You get a polished dashboard and less infra to think about. Strong fit if the majority of your work is WordPress. Compare Kinsta.

Mostly WordPress → Kinsta. Mix of WP and custom apps → Cloudways. For a tailored recommendation based on your workload and spend, run our free FinOps audit.
