Verdict: Cloudways for full-stack flexibility; Kinsta for WordPress excellence

If your workload is exclusively WordPress — especially agencies running WooCommerce or high-traffic content sites — Kinsta's managed platform, 99.9% SLA, and WordPress-expert support are worth the premium. For DevOps teams, multi-app servers, or anyone running non-WordPress workloads alongside WordPress, Cloudways offers better value — unlimited apps per server, 5 cloud providers to choose from, and no per-site pricing that adds up fast on Kinsta.

FAQ

Is Cloudways cheaper than Kinsta?

For single sites, Kinsta starts at $35/mo vs Cloudways $11/mo — Cloudways is cheaper. For multiple sites the gap grows significantly: 10 sites on Kinsta costs $230+/mo, while Cloudways can host all 10 on one server for $24–46/mo. For agencies, Cloudways is substantially cheaper at scale.

Which is better for WordPress performance — Cloudways or Kinsta?

Both deliver excellent WordPress performance. Kinsta's Google Cloud C3D machines with built-in CDN and WP-specific caching are purpose-built for WordPress and require zero tuning. Cloudways with Object Cache Pro and the Cloudflare add-on is comparable — but requires more configuration. For hands-off WP performance, Kinsta edges ahead.

Can I run non-WordPress apps on Cloudways?

Yes. Cloudways supports PHP, Node.js, Python, Laravel, Magento, Drupal, and custom stacks alongside WordPress. Kinsta is WordPress-only — you cannot host other app types on Kinsta.

Does Kinsta have a free trial?

Kinsta does not offer a free trial but provides a 30-day money-back guarantee. Cloudways offers a 3-day free trial with no credit card required.

Which has better support — Cloudways or Kinsta?

Both offer 24/7 live chat. Kinsta's support team specializes exclusively in WordPress — ideal if you're having WP-specific issues. Cloudways support covers server management and general stack questions. For WordPress-specific help, Kinsta's team is more specialized.

Can I migrate from Cloudways to Kinsta (or vice versa)?

Yes. Kinsta offers free premium migrations from any host including Cloudways. Moving to Cloudways can be done with the free All-in-One WP Migration plugin or their migration service. Both directions are well-documented.
