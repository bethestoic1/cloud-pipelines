FAQ

Does Cloudways charge per site or per server?

Per server. You can host unlimited WordPress installs, PHP apps, and other applications on a single Cloudways server. This is the key pricing advantage over Kinsta, which charges per WordPress installation.

What is the cheapest Cloudways plan?

$11/mo for the DigitalOcean 1 GB plan. It includes 1 vCPU, 25 GB SSD storage, and 1 TB bandwidth. Suitable for small blogs, dev environments, and 1–3 low-traffic WordPress sites.

Does Cloudways offer a free trial?

Yes — Cloudways offers a 3-day free trial with no credit card required. You can deploy a server and test the platform before committing.

Does the price change at renewal?

No. Cloudways does not use introductory pricing. The rate you see is the rate you pay month after month. This is a significant advantage over SiteGround, which renews at 3–6× the introductory price.

What is the best Cloudways plan for a WordPress agency?

For most agencies with 5–15 client sites, the DigitalOcean 2 GB plan at $24/mo is the sweet spot. It supports unlimited sites, includes Object Cache Pro free, and provides enough RAM for typical PHP workloads. Scale to the 4 GB plan ($46/mo) if you run WooCommerce or high-traffic sites.

Which cloud provider should I choose on Cloudways?

DigitalOcean is the best default choice — cheapest per GB of RAM, 15 data center regions, and well-optimized for WordPress. Choose AWS if you need maximum global reach or compliance requirements. Choose GCP if you want the same infrastructure as Kinsta at Cloudways prices. Vultr and Linode/Akamai are good alternatives at similar or lower cost.

Can I switch cloud providers after deployment?

Not directly. To switch providers (e.g. from DigitalOcean to AWS), you would clone your applications to a new server on the target provider and then point your DNS. Cloudways makes this straightforward with their server cloning tool.
