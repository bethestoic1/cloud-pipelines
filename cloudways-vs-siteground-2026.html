Verdict: Cloudways for agencies; SiteGround for beginners and small sites

For agencies and developers running multiple WordPress or mixed-stack sites, Cloudways wins on raw value — unlimited sites per server, no surprise renewal hikes, and full cloud provider choice. For solo bloggers or small businesses launching their first site on a tight budget, SiteGround's $2.99/mo introductory plan includes CDN, email, and daily backups — hard to beat for year one.

Pros

Who should pick Cloudways?

Agencies running 5–50+ WordPress or WooCommerce sites, developers who need SSH and CLI access, and anyone who wants predictable monthly billing with no surprise renewal hikes. At $11/mo you can host unlimited sites — SiteGround would charge $35.99+/mo at renewal for the same number of sites on GrowBig.

Who should pick SiteGround?

Bloggers, freelancers, and small businesses launching their first WordPress site. SiteGround's $2.99/mo intro plan bundles everything you need (CDN, SSL, email, daily backups, auto-updates) in one low price. Just know the renewal rate — budget for ~$17.99/mo after year one on StartUp or ~$29.99/mo on GrowBig.

FAQ

Is Cloudways cheaper than SiteGround long-term?

Yes, for most users after the first year. SiteGround's $2.99/mo is introductory only — it renews at $17.99/mo (StartUp) or $29.99/mo (GrowBig). Cloudways starts at $11/mo with no price hike. For agencies with multiple sites, Cloudways is significantly cheaper over 2+ years.

Which is faster — Cloudways or SiteGround?

Both are fast for WordPress. SiteGround's SuperCacher and included Cloudflare CDN deliver excellent out-of-the-box performance. Cloudways with Object Cache Pro and Cloudflare is comparable or better for high-traffic sites. For a basic blog, the difference is negligible.

Does SiteGround include email hosting?

Yes — SiteGround includes free email hosting on all plans. Cloudways does not include email; you need a separate service (Google Workspace, Zoho, etc.).

Can I migrate from SiteGround to Cloudways?

Yes. Cloudways offers a free WordPress migration plugin and migration service. Moving from SiteGround to Cloudways is a common upgrade path as sites grow beyond shared hosting limits.

Does Cloudways offer a free trial?

Yes — Cloudways offers a 3-day free trial with no credit card required. SiteGround offers a 30-day money-back guarantee.
